This is keg.io


keg.io is a community of Arduino-powered kegerators hooked up to a Node.js server in the cloud that fosters consumption of tasty beer with friends local or remote.

Earn badges and compete with your friends:

  • Party Starter: be the first drinker of the day
  • Beer Aficionado: pour beers from five consecutive kegs
  • Power User: drink a beer each day for five consecutive days
  • and lots more...

Signup if you have your unique keg.io ID, or select a kegerator on the left to see who's drinking.
